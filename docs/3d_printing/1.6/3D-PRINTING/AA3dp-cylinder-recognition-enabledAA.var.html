*3DP-CYLINDER-RECOGNITION-ENABLED* [Public Variable]

Package: 3d-printing (Nick name(s): :3dp)

Cylinder recognition option default. Can be customized in 3dp_customize.lsp.

If set to T the cylinder recognition component attempts to find best-fit cylindrical regions for sets of contiguous mesh triangles with a given approximation accuracy. The planar regions are then converted into cylindrical model faces to reduce model complexity.

Specify NIL to turn off recognition of cylindrical faces by default.
